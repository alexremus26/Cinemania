Calculator Preț Bilet

Formula Preț Bilet:

P final = P base × ( 1 − r ) + taxa

r = rata de reducere (ex: student r = 0.20, senior r = 0.30, fără reducere r = 0).

Exemplu rapid (Pbase=30, taxa=1): student = 25 RON, senior = 22 RON, standard = 31 RON.
